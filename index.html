If n! is defined as the product of all positive integers from 1 to n, then: 1! = 1*1 = 1 2! = 1*2 = 2 3! = 1*2*3 = 6 4! = 1*2*3*4 = 24 ... n! = 1*2*3*...*(n-2)*(n-1)*n and so on. Logically, n! can also be expressed n*(n-1)! . Therefore, at n=1, using n! = n*(n-1)! 1! = 1*0! which simplifies to 1 = 0!
